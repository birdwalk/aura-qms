# Internal Audit Procedures Guide (Synthetic — Demo Only)

## Purpose
This guide describes the internal audit process for organisations pursuing ISO 9001:2015
certification. Internal audits verify that the QMS is implemented, maintained, and effective.

## Audit Programme Planning
- Audits must be planned at regular intervals (typically annually, with more frequent
  audits for high-risk or previously non-conforming areas)
- The audit programme shall consider the importance of the processes concerned, changes
  affecting the organisation, and the results of previous audits
- Audit criteria, scope, frequency, and methods shall be defined

## Conducting an Audit
**Opening Meeting**
Confirm scope, objectives, and timetable with the auditee. Explain the audit process.

**Document Review**
Review relevant documented information including policies, procedures, and records
before on-site activities.

**On-Site Activities**
- Interview process owners and staff
- Observe processes and activities
- Verify records and evidence
- Seek objective evidence for each requirement being audited

**Findings Classification**
- **Major Nonconformity (Major NC)**: Absence or total breakdown of a system to meet
  a requirement. Likely to result in the failure to achieve the intended outcome.
- **Minor Nonconformity (Minor NC)**: A single observed lapse, isolated failure, or
  partial breakdown of a system requirement.
- **Observation / OFI**: A statement of fact not yet constituting a nonconformity but
  presenting risk of becoming one, or an opportunity to improve.

## Corrective Action Process
1. Auditee acknowledges the finding
2. Root cause analysis performed (5-Why, fishbone, or similar method)
3. Corrective action plan submitted within agreed timeframe
4. Action implemented and evidence collected
5. Effectiveness verification at follow-up audit
6. Finding closed upon satisfactory verification

## Audit Report Requirements
The audit report shall include:
- Audit objectives and scope
- Audit team members
- Dates and locations
- Summary of findings (NC count by type, OFIs)
- Conclusions regarding audit objective achievement
- Distribution list
